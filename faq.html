FAQ

Questions

Aren’t you just another crowdfunding platform?

Not exactly. Unlike most crowdfunding platforms, we provide a full fundraising service for scientists by coaching them on their pitch, filming it, and sharing it with target audiences. Scientists will be able to accumulate a monthly research income to conduct their research over the long term rather than raising funds for one short-term project on their own. Scientists will also not be in danger of losing pledged donations by pre-set deadlines. Donors will be able to make ongoing donations with the option of canceling their subscription mid-way if they are unhappy or unable to continue their donation.

When will the Beta be launched?

We are shooting for early 2019, and hope to begin working with scientists to prepare for this launch throughout 2018.

Are scientists from all career stages eligible to participate?

For the beta platform, we are recruiting early career scientists from the PhD level through (and including) the rank of assistant professor or lecturer.

Which fields of science are you recruiting from?

For the beta platform, we are recruiting early career scientists from the natural sciences (e.g. medicine, physics, chemistry, biology, earth science and related fields). We aim to begin working with social scientists after the beta run. Please be in touch if you are interested in getting involved in the future!

Which countries are you recruiting from?

Currently, we are limited to scientists working in the US and UK (though you do not need to be a national of those countries). For the beta platform, we are recruiting specifically from New York and London, and hope to expand from there. Please still be in touch if you are interested in getting involved in the future but aren’t located in NYC or London. This can help us figure out where to focus our energies next.

How can scientists apply to be featured on the platform?

If you are interested in participating in the beta, please fill out our beta contact form. While we cannot guarantee a spot on the beta platform, we will do our best to accommodate interested candidates. A more streamlined application procedure will be introduced after the beta. Scientists’ feedback during the beta will be crucial in helping us develop the most effective selection procedures in the future.

What do you look for in a scientist who wishes to participate on the platform?

We don’t have hard and fast criteria but will give preference to those working on exploratory (basic) and risky science, as well as research topics that have failed or typically fail to get funding. A lot also depends on the scientists who reach out to us. They should have a deep passion for their work and a desire to have a long-term impact with their research. They do not need to be an extrovert or great public speaker to get behind camera to make the pitch. We’ll help with that part!

How much work am I required to put in as a scientist?

You should be ready to spend one or two afternoons with us for our coaching and pitch filming sessions, preferably in your lab or university. We’ll then take it over from there as we develop a digital profile summarizing your research and share your video pitch with the public. You’ll have a say in everything we do, but we take care of all the legwork. Every month we’ll ask you to update your donor base with a progress report. We can also work with you individually to come up with creative ways of sharing your research and increase your involvement if you desire.

Can I apply as an independent scientist outside of a university?

At the moment, no. We are only considering early career scientists employed in an official research position at an accredited university with endorsements from senior scientists in their field.

How will you help share scientists’ research with the public?

Our aim is to help scientists throughout the fundraising process. This involves coaching scientists on their pitches, capturing those pitches on beautiful film, creating a compelling digital profile where each scientist’s research can be accessible to lay audiences, crafting a digital marketing strategy for each scientist, and even helping scientists deliver public lectures in exchange for public donations to their research. Our dedicated team will tailor an outreach strategy for each scientist’s particular needs.

How can you guarantee that the best science is represented on the platform?

We certainly can’t guarantee that the science on the platform will be revolutionary or groundbreaking, but we want to give talented young scientists the opportunity to pitch their research and express their passion. To prove their qualifications, all scientists on our platform must have two endorsements from senior scientists in their field. These endorsements will appear on each scientist’s public profile on the platform.

How can we be sure the donated money isn’t misused?

Scientists will provide regular progress updates to their donor base every month by posting notes or video diaries from their lab. You’ll be able to see these updates directly on the platform.

I’m a scientist hoping to spin off my research into a commercial enterprise. Can I use your platform to get seed funding?

At the moment, our model is purely philanthropy based. You can use our platform to raise seed funds for the research and proof of concept stages of your spinoff while you are still employed at a university. We will not be able to broker a contract between you and an investor to develop your enterprise, but we hope to develop ways of generating those initial opportunities for you as we grow.

I’m an investor. Can I use your platform to invest in early stage spinoffs?

At the moment, our model is purely philanthropy based, so we cannot facilitate a financial return for your ongoing donation. Most research on the platform will consist of exploratory, basic or high-risk science that tends to be poorly funded and isn’t necessarily commercially viable. While the payoffs of investing into this kind of science are not immediate or financial, they might yield significant benefits for humanity over the long-term. It’s a different kind of investment, and we hope you’ll find it a worthy one!

I’m a philanthropist interested in science. How can we work together?

We are interested in building partnerships with philanthropists to develop a sustainable support network for scientists on our platform. Contact us to discuss how you can be a proactive part of our community and get the most impact out of each dollar.

I’m a business person interested in having more social impact. How can we get involved?

We’d love to hear from businesses interested in funding scientists. Contact us to discuss opportunities to collaborate and get the most social impact out of an ongoing company donation on our platform.

Can I join your team?

We’re always on the lookout for new team members passionate about our mission. Contact us and let us know how you’d like to contribute.
